Privacy Policy

Thank you for showing interest in Swell App (“app,” “Swell,” “we,” “us” or “our”).

At Swell App, we value and respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. We are committed to ensuring the confidentiality and security of your data. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

Data Storage

Our app allows you to create and store diary entries containing your thoughts and emotions. These entries are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers or any third parties. You have full control over your diary content and can delete entries at any time — it will be done automatically when you remove Swell from your device. Swell can be used without sharing any personal identifiable information.

To understand your needs, provide you with insights and to improve our services we may collect depersonalized data regarding your app usage, such as app launches, in-app purchases, features usage, or other information about how users interact with our app. This information is used solely for analytical purposes and cannot be linked to your personal identity.

Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users who are at least 16 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 16, we will take steps to delete that information.

Nevertheless, if the law mandates a higher age threshold for the app to offer its services to you without requiring parental consent, you must meet this specified minimum age.
